About Me

Experience Overview

I started working with computer hardware from the age of 7 when I still lived in a country called Belarus. I was helping my uncle fulfill custom computer orders for his customers. By the age of 15 I began playing around with video game development. I self taught my self C# and worked with the Unity engine. I later picked up Unreal engine which forced me to learn C++. Freshman year of High school I learned a language called AutoIt which allowed me to create desktop bots to automate different tasks. Throught my Junior and Senior year in high school I took several computer science classes and further learned C++, Web Development and Python. After High School I went straight into University to pursue a Computer Science degree. Currently I am a Junior and have taken several CS classes where I learned about algorithms, data structure, computer hardware, and programming. I have been working at Code Central as a Programming Instructor where I help students learn and accomplish different programming/robotic projects. I have also had the opportunity to be a Technical Advisor for Scary Robot during one of their video game releases.
